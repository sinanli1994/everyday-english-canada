    About this project

    Everyday English Canada is a small phrasebook for newcomers, international students, and visitors. Instead of textbook sentences, it collects the polite, natural expressions Canadians actually use — at the grocery store, in restaurants, at the clinic, and when renting a home. Each phrase comes with a Chinese translation and, where helpful, a short note about local customs.
